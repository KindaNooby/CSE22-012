Plot

In Gotham City, a younger version of Batman is still early in his crime-fighting career. The film takes a darker and grittier tone, delving into the detective aspect of Batman's character. As Batman investigates a series of crimes plaguing the city, he uncovers a conspiracy that puts Gotham at risk and brings him face-to-face with a rogues' gallery of villains, including the enigmatic Riddler, portrayed by Paul Dano. The film aims to provide a fresh and grounded take on the Batman mythos, exploring the character's psychology and the morally complex world he inhabits.
