Plot

A mythic and emotionally charged hero’s journey, Dune tells the story of Paul Atreides, a brilliant and gifted man born into a great destiny beyond his understanding, who must travel to the most dangerous planet in the universe to ensure the future of his family and his people.

As manevolent forces explode into conflict over the planets exclusive supply of the most precious resource in existence-a commodity capable of unlocking humanity’s greatest potential-only those who conquer their fear will survive.
